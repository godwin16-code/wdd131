EcoTravel Adventures

A website dedicated to eco-friendly travel guides and adventure planning.

Site Purpose

The site provides eco-conscious travelers with information about sustainable destinations, travel tips, and interactive tools like quizzes and estimators for planning responsible trips.

Scenarios

  • What are the best eco-friendly destinations for a family vacation?
  • How can I calculate my travel carbon footprint for a planned trip?

Color Schema

Secondary Color: #F39C12 – buttons and highlights.

Typography

  • Body: 'Tahoma', sans-serif
